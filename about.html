About Ozamiz

Ozamiz is a charming city located in Misamis Occidental, Philippines. It is surrounded by stunning natural landscapes, including the beautiful Panguil Bay, the lush Mount Malindang, and scenic rivers. Known for its peaceful environment and friendly people, Ozamiz offers a mix of historical landmarks, cultural heritage, and modern amenities that make it a unique place to visit or live in.

History

Ozamiz has a rich history that dates back to the Spanish colonial period. Originally, it was a small community centered around Fort Santiago, also called Cotta Fort, which was built in the 1700s to protect the area from pirate attacks. The city was later named after Jose Ozamiz, a Filipino hero who fought for freedom during World War II. Over the years, Ozamiz has grown into a lively city that honors its past while moving forward into the future.
