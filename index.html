Welcome to Ozamiz

Ozamiz is a vibrant city in the Philippines located in Misamis Occidental, known for its rich history and natural beauty. It is home to historical landmarks like the Ozamiz Fort and stunning attractions like Mount Malindang and the Panguil Bay. The city offers a mix of cultural heritage and modern amenities, making it a charming destination for travelers.

Mission

To foster sustainable growth and development in Ozamiz City by preserving its rich heritage, promoting tourism, and enhancing the quality of life for its residents through inclusive and innovative programs.

Vission

To be a progressive and culturally vibrant city, recognized as a premier destination in Mindanao for its historical significance, natural beauty, and thriving community.

Core Values

Core Values We uphold integrity by ensuring honesty and transparency in all our actions, embrace inclusivity by fostering a community where diversity is valued and opportunities are accessible to everyone, strive for innovation by seeking creative solutions to address challenges and improve lives, commit to sustainability by protecting our environment and resources for future generations, and aim for excellence by consistently delivering high-quality services and initiatives for the betterment of our community.
